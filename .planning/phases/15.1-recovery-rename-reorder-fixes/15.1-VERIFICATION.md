**Expected:** Recovery panel shows flat full-width rows (not cards with borders/rounded corners). **Expected:** Rename is cancelled immediately on first press, original title is restored (typed text discarded), and cursor returns to the editor.
**Why human:** Keyboard event tunnel behavior and focus routing need interactive verification.

**Expected:** The dragged tab fades to 50% opacity in place (no ghost following cursor). Drop indicator lines appear at valid positions. On release, the tab appears at its new position and smoothly fades from 50% to 100% opacity over ~150ms.
**Why human:** Animation timing, opacity visual appearance, and overall drag feel need interactive testing.

**Expected:** Only 5 tab previews shown, followed by "+N more" in muted italic text.
